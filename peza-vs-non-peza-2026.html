Quick Refresher: What Is PEZA?

PEZA = Philippine Economic Zone Authority
Companies registered with PEZA (IT-BPM, KPO, software development, shared services, etc.) unlock powerful tax incentives — but only when they lease inside PEZA-accredited buildings.

So… Who Actually Keeps More Money?

Case 1: You’re PEZA-registered (BPO, IT-BPM, KPO, software house)

Team PEZA takes the clear win.

Even with ₱150–200 extra per sqm, the tax advantages deliver massive savings.

Real example (1,000 sqm office, ₱60M gross income):

  • Additional rent ≈ ₱1.8–2.4M per year
  • Tax savings ≈ ₱12–16M per year
  • Net gain of ₱10M+ annually — game-changing.

Case 2: Local trading, consulting, traditional corporation

Team Non-PEZA is the smart play.

You enjoy lower base rents and still get great locations and amenities.

Case 3: Startup planning to register with PEZA in the next 12–18 months

Join Team PEZA from day one.

Premium PEZA floors are moving faster than ever in 2026 — secure the space now and activate the incentives once registration is approved (often with retroactive effect).

Smart Details Most People Appreciate Knowing

  • Some buildings are only partially PEZA-accredited (specific floors only) — always request the official certificate to confirm full coverage.
  • Parking rates: ₱4,500–6,000/slot in Northgate & Filinvest Cyberzone vs ₱2,500–4,000 in non-PEZA zones.
  • Fit-out allowance: PEZA buildings typically offer 3–6 months rent-free; non-PEZA usually 1–3 months — great for bigger build-outs.
