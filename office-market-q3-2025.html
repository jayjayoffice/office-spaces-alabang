Alabang Office Market Q3 2025: The Ultimate Guide to Finding Your Next Workspace

08 September 2025

Welcome to your essential guide to the Alabang office market for Q3 2025. If you’re searching for the right space for your business, this report breaks down the latest trends, rental rates, and available options across Alabang’s prime business districts.

Filinvest Corporate City: Premium Spaces with Flexibility

For businesses that thrive in a modern and dynamic environment, Filinvest Corporate City continues to be a top choice. Rental rates average ₱700 per sqm, with spaces ranging from compact 24 sqm suites—ideal for startups—to expansive 797 sqm floors for growing enterprises. Its central location and flexibility make it one of the most in-demand districts this quarter.

Madrigal Business Park: Value and Accessibility Combined

Madrigal Business Park offers excellent value for companies looking for a prime address without premium costs. With rates starting as low as ₱510 per sqm, it’s a cost-efficient alternative for businesses of all sizes. Options range from 200 sqm offices to full 876 sqm floors, making it a practical choice for firms balancing budget and convenience.

Northgate Cyberzone: A Hub for Tech and BPO Growth

As the leading hub for technology and outsourcing firms, Northgate Cyberzone is built for efficiency, scalability, and innovation. Rental rates fall between ₱700–₱880 per sqm, reflecting its high demand. Large-format spaces are widely available this quarter, with 1,700–2,300 sqm floors catering to companies requiring substantial operations space.

Market Snapshot

  • Filinvest Corporate City: Premium, flexible office options.
  • Madrigal Business Park: Competitive rates and a balanced environment.
  • Northgate Cyberzone: Large footprints for tech-driven enterprises.
