Why Alabang?

A Strategic Location for Growth

Alabang is a gem for any business looking to grow. Tucked in southern Metro Manila, it’s got easy access to highways like SLEX and is close to the airport—perfect for connecting with clients. Plus, its residential vibe means shorter commutes for your team. It’s a friendly, strategic spot that blends convenience with opportunity.

A Thriving Business Ecosystem

Alabang stands out with its vibrant mix of modern offices like Filinvest City and Northgate Cyberzone, green spaces, and lifestyle spots. It’s a hub that attracts tech startups, large corporations, and creative teams, providing excellent networking and talent opportunities. The nearby Westgate Center and Alabang Town Center offer cozy cafés and upscale dining options for brainstorming sessions or client meetings. It’s a lively setting that boosts both work and enjoyment.

Quality of Life Meets Professional Appeal

Alabang offers a refreshing mix of work and relaxation. It’s calmer than central Manila, with green spaces and parks like River Park to unwind. Your team can enjoy a quick break or weekend outings at local malls, keeping spirits high. The office options here are impressive yet budget-friendly—a great pick for anyone aiming to settle in comfortably.

Available Spaces

Spectrum at Filinvest Corporate City

At the heart of the city’s business landscape, the Spectrum District stands as its economic core. Surrounded by major roads—Corporate Avenue, Commerce Avenue, and Filinvest Avenue—it serves as the central office hub, home to top local and multinational companies. The district also features upscale residences, premier hotels, and vibrant commercial spaces that reflect the dynamic spirit of Filinvest City as a premier central business district.

Northgate Cyberzone

Northgate Cyberzone is an 18.7-hectare, PEZA-registered IT park located at the heart of Filinvest City. Dubbed the BPO and IT hub of the South, it offers a dynamic, campus-style environment tailored to the needs of tech-driven businesses. It’s home to a wide range of industries—including BPOs, KPOs, call centers, fintech, e-commerce, and creative tech such as software and multimedia design. With its strategic location, robust infrastructure, and 24/7 operations, Northgate Cyberzone continues to attract top-tier companies and talent from across the metro.

Madrigal Business Park

Madrigal Business Park is a master-planned commercial district in Alabang, Muntinlupa. Known for its wide roads, green open spaces, and low-density layout, it offers a calm yet connected business environment. Strategically located near major roads and lifestyle hubs like Alabang Town Center, it is highly accessible. Home to corporate offices, banks, and upscale retail, it blends business convenience with lifestyle appeal—making Madrigal Business Park a preferred address for companies seeking a premium location in the South.
